üìã IDE Transformation Checklist

Transform your workflow from SME to IDE thinking. Check off each item as you go!

  • Day 1: Set up your AI assistant Sign up for Claude.ai (free) or ChatGPT. Start using AI for brainstorming and code review.
  • Day 2-3: Install developer AI tools Set up GitHub Copilot or Cursor IDE. Let AI help you write code faster.
  • Week 1: Start a side experiment Pick a problem you care about. Build a quick prototype in one weekend with AI help.
  • Week 2: Practice rapid iteration Ship something small to real users. Get feedback. Improve. Repeat.
  • Week 3: Embrace failure as data Try something that might not work. Document what you learn regardless of outcome.
  • Week 4: Build in public Share your progress on Twitter/LinkedIn. Connect with others building cool things.
  • Month 2: Cross-functional collaboration Partner with someone outside your skill set. Designer? Marketer? Business person?
  • Month 3: 10x thinking exercise Take one of your ideas. Ask: "How would I make this 10x bigger/better/faster?"

üìñ Reading List

Expand your innovation mindset with these resources.

  • "Zero to One" by Peter Thiel The classic on creating new things vs. copying existing ones. Core IDE philosophy.
  • "The Lean Startup" by Eric Ries Build-measure-learn cycles. MVP thinking. Validated learning.
  • "Thinking in Bets" by Annie Duke Decision-making under uncertainty. Treating failures as data.
  • "The Mom Test" by Rob Fitzpatrick How to talk to users and learn what to build. Essential for experimentation.
  • Paul Graham's Essays (paulgraham.com) Free! Ideas on startups, innovation, and building things that matter.
